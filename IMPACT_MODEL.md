\## Assumptions \& Risks



\### Key Assumptions



1\. \*\*Market Size:\*\* 30L active ET users willing to pay

&#x20;  - \*Basis:\* ET Prime has 10L subs; intelligence is stickier

2\. \*\*Pricing:\*\* ₹1,499/month acceptable

&#x20;  - \*Basis:\* 30% of ET Prime (₹999/month); adds value

3\. \*\*Conversion:\*\* 1% of free users to paid

&#x20;  - \*Basis:\* Industry standard for SaaS products

4\. \*\*Retention:\*\* 80% annual retention

&#x20;  - \*Basis:\* Similar to ET Prime (high switching cost)

5\. \*\*OpenAI costs:\*\* ₹3/1000 tokens

&#x20;  - \*Basis:\* GPT-4o-mini pricing (Dec 2025)



\### Risk Mitigation



| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |

|------|-------------|--------|------------|

| Low conversion | Medium | High | Free tier + 14-day trial |

| OpenAI cost spike | Low | Medium | Cache 90% of queries; mock fallback |

| Competitor copy | High | Medium | ET moat (data exclusivity) |

| Regulatory (SEBI) | Low | High | Disclaimer: "Not investment advice" |
